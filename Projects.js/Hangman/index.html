Welcome to Hangman Game!



The purpose of the game:
Guess the hidden word by choosing letters from the alphabet, while trying to avoid mistakes that will promote the hanging of the figure.


Category selection:
At the beginning of the game, the player must choose a category from a list (for example: animals, countries, fruits , etc.).Once you select a category, you wont be able to change another one untill you finish the selected one.


Introducing the hidden word:
After selecting the category, the hidden word will be displayed as a panel of dashes, with each dash representing a letter (eg: _ _ _ _).


Guessing letters:
The player chooses a letter each turn. If the letter is present in the word, it will be revealed in the appropriate place on the board. If the letter does not exist in the word, a step is advanced in the hanging of the figure (a hand, a leg, etc. is added).


Displaying character status:
The game will show a "hanging man" figure, where each mistake adds another part to the figure (head, body, hands, feet).


win or lose:
Win = If the player manages to guess all the letters in the word before the hanging is complete. And so complete all the categories in the game. Loss = If all parts of the figure are drawn before the word is fully revealed.
