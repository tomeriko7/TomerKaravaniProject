Game instructions:


Preparations for the game:
Click the "Start Game" button to start a new game. Each time you press the button, the colors on the cards will be redistributed randomly. All cards will start in the closed position (their backs are visible).


The course of the game:
Click on a card to turn it over and find out its suit. Then, click on another card to try to find a matching pair. If the suit of the two cards matches, they will remain visible. If the suits don't match, the cards will flip back to the back side after 1-2 seconds.


End game:
The game ends when all the cards on the board are faced up and matched into pairs.


Additions and special rules:
Restart button:
You can press the "Start Game" button at any moment to start a new game. The button will redistribute the colors on the board and restart the game.

Remember the colors:
Use your memory to remember where each color is, and try to match pairs as fast as possible. You can open a stopwatch.


Additional highlights:
The game is suitable for all ages and improves memory and concentration skills.
